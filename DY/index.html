Our Team Profiles

Pastor Olufowokan Adeoye John

Founder & CEO, WEKIA Foundation

A visionary leader, passionate humanitarian, and transformational coach committed to building lives and empowering communities through faith, service, and social impact. Graduate of Computer Science from Olabisi Onabanjo University (2006) and member of the Chartered Insurance Institute of Nigeria (CIIN). Founder of WEKIA Foundation with a mission to empower women, educate children, and support vulnerable individuals.

Pastor Samson Abiola Olojede

Vice Chairman, Board of Trustees, WEKIA Foundation

Distinguished leader with a rich background in public service and spiritual ministry. Served in Nigeria Police Force for nearly fifteen years. National Pastor in Living Faith Church Worldwide. Leads humanitarian initiatives including community outreach, educational support, medical aid, and youth rehabilitation. Member of the National Institute of Marketing of Nigeria (MNIMN).

Mrs Alice Folasade Obayomi

Executive Secretary, WEKIA Foundation Board of Trustees

Retired seasoned Nurse & Midwife with over 35 years of service. Known for commitment to maternal and child health, emergency care, and community wellness. Mentored many young nurses and midwives. Brings grassroots healthcare knowledge to WEKIA Foundation’s health initiatives.

Mr John Daniel Nafarnda

Chairman, Advisory Board, WEKIA Foundation

Fellow of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria (FCNA) and other professional bodies. Over 9 years experience in NGOs as Head of Finance, Internal Auditor, and Project Accountant. Passionate about transparency and accountability in non-profits.

Barr. Ihemere Chiemela Tony

Member of Advisory Board, WEKIA Foundation

Distinguished legal practitioner with nearly two decades of experience. Principal Partner at Legal Draft Consults. Active in legal reform and community legal awareness. Advocates for accessible and inclusive legal system.

Mrs. Sonaike Ruth Oluwaseyi

Member, Advisory Board – WEKIA Foundation

Accomplished professional in economics, finance, education, and entrepreneurship. Former banker and UK care sector team lead. Passionate about humanitarian service and empowering underprivileged individuals.
